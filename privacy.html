我们如何处理你的资料

Pluto 默认在设备上计算、生图并保存本地历史。云端图谱保存和匿名产品统计是两个独立开关，均默认关闭。关闭时，姓名、出生日期、出生时间、地点和完整图谱不会上传到 Pluto 后台。

只有在你明确开启“将新生成的说明书保存到云端”后，新生成的姓名、出生资料、时区、图谱快照及版本信息才会上传。“帮助我们改进 Pluto”只发送经白名单限制的操作事件，禁止姓名、出生资料、地点、完整图谱、自由文本和错误堆栈。

为了把你输入的出生地点转换为计算所需的坐标和时区，应用会把你主动输入的地点搜索文字发送给 Photon；服务不可用时可能使用 ArcGIS Geocoding 作为备用。我们不会随地点文字一同发送姓名、出生日期或生成结果。第三方服务可能按照各自政策记录网络请求。

设备权限

  • 相册：仅在你点击“保存图片”时请求，用于把生成图片写入系统相册。
  • 系统分享：仅在你主动分享时调用，由 Apple 的分享面板决定接收方。

保留与删除

本地历史会保留到你清空、关闭保存或卸载应用。“删除云端图谱与个人资料”会在同一事务中删除当前匿名身份保存的姓名、出生资料、图谱、授权记录与云端资料，不会删除本设备历史。已经记录的匿名使用事件会移除用户标识，并最多保留 180 天用于汇总统计；不含出生资料的删除收据最多保留 365 天。两类删除需要分别操作。

儿童与健康声明

本应用面向 13 岁及以上用户，用于自我探索与娱乐，不提供医疗、心理、法律或财务诊断与建议。

ENGLISH

How we handle your information

Pluto calculates and renders locally by default. Cloud chart saving and anonymous product analytics are separate switches and are both off by default. While off, your name, birth date, birth time, location, and complete chart are not uploaded to the Pluto backend.

Only after you explicitly enable cloud saving may new names, birth details, time zones, snapshots, and versions be uploaded. Help Us Improve Pluto sends only allowlisted action events and rejects names, birth details, locations, complete charts, free text, and raw stacks.

To convert a place you enter into coordinates and a time zone, the app sends only your place-search text to Photon and may use ArcGIS Geocoding as a fallback. We do not send your name, birth date, or generated result with that query. These providers may process network requests under their own policies.

Device permissions

  • Photos: requested only when you choose Save Image, solely to add the generated image to your library.
  • System sharing: opened only when you choose Share; recipients are controlled by Apple's share sheet.

Retention and deletion

Local history remains until you clear it, turn history off, or uninstall. Delete Cloud Charts and Personal Data atomically removes the current anonymous identity's saved name, birth details, charts, consent records, and cloud profile, but not local history. Previously recorded usage events are deidentified and retained for no more than 180 days for aggregate statistics. Birth-data-free deletion receipts are retained for no more than 365 days. Local and cloud deletion are independent.

Children and wellness disclaimer

The app is intended for ages 13 and older. It is for personal reflection and entertainment and does not provide medical, psychological, legal, or financial diagnosis or advice.
